The Appalachian Trail offers endless hiking opportunities from Georgia to Maine; plan your visit now! Our mission is to make the wilderness accessible and enjoyable to all. We believe the trail is there for everyone at every phase of life and we welcome ALL hikers regardless of their background or ability level. We strive to provide safe on-trail experiences with engaging, knowledgeable, and well-equipped guides. We follow Leave No Trace principles to emphasize the importance of enjoying the outdoors responsibly.

The Appalachian Trail is a 2,180+ mile long footpath that traverses the scenic, wooded, pastoral, wild, and culturally resonant lands of the Appalachian Mountains. This beautiful trail stretches from Georgia to Maine!

General Information

At Nature & Beyond we strive to ensure our guests enjoy themselves and their safety while enjoying these wonderful experiences.

With over 1000 miles of permitted trail - Nature & Beyond Hiking Company offers a wide array of Overnight options. Our guides are expert backpackers who share a love for the outdoors - and a love for getting people into the outdoors. And, all of our guided trips include the use of the highest quality Ultralight Gear.


EXPECTATIONS

  1. Plan to carry all necessary gear and leave extra items behind (your guide will inspect your gear before you hike).
  2. Tell the hike leaders of any medical conditions, dietary needs, or personal concerns before the trip.
  3. Expect to walk 6-8 hours per day over moderate to difficult terrain.
  4. Come with a good attitude and a group mantality. This will be an amazing and potentially life-changing experience!
